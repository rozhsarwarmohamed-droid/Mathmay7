🎯

Learning Objectives

  • 📏
    Choose the right unit of time for different activities (minutes, hours, days, weeks, months, years).
  • 🗓️
    Read a calendar and count the days between two dates correctly.
  • 🧮
    Calculate time intervals — adding and subtracting weeks and days.
📖

Key Vocabulary

These are the important words for today:

calendar date days between weeks months years interval duration
💡

Important Reminder!

Days BETWEEN two dates: Do NOT count the two dates themselves.

Example: Days between Monday and Wednesday = just Tuesday = 1 day.

Questions
2a

How many days are between 22nd January and 18th February?

Look at the 🟠 22 Jan (start) and 🩷 18 Feb (end).
Count the days in between — do not count those two days!

Hint: Count remaining January days + February days up to (but not including) the 18th.
✓ Answer
26 days
Jan 23–31 = 9 days  +  Feb 1–17 = 17 days  =  26 days total.
2b

What is the date three weeks after 1st March?

3 weeks = 21 days.
Start at March 1st and count forward 21 days.

✓ Answer
22nd March
1 March + 21 days = 22nd March. (Look at the orange square on the March calendar!)
2c

Carnival day is 17th March. Plans are made six weeks before. What date do they start planning?

6 weeks = 42 days.
Go backwards 42 days from 17th March.

Hint: 17 March → go back 17 days = 1 March, then go back 25 more days into February.
✓ Answer
3rd February
42 days before 17 Mar: go back 17 days → Mar 1, then 25 more days → Feb 3rd.
2d

Zalika plans a holiday to last three weeks. She leaves on 19th January. When does she arrive back?

3 weeks = 21 days.
Add 21 days to 19th January.

Hint: January has 31 days. 31 − 19 = 12 days left in January. Then continue into February.
✓ Answer
9th February
19 Jan + 12 days = 31 Jan, then 9 more days → 9th February.

Question 3 — Travel & Stays

Use what you know about days, nights, and weeks.

3a

Many airlines ask you to buy a ticket 14 days before your flight. If Thandiwe leaves on 20th June, on what day should he buy his ticket?

Count 14 days backwards from 20th June.

Hint: 20 − 14 = ?
✓ Answer
Monday, 6th June
20 − 14 = 6th June. Looking at the June 2022 calendar, the 6th is a Monday.
3b

Misha arrives at the hotel on 8th July and will stay three nights. What date will Misha check out?

If you arrive on the 8th and sleep 3 nights, you leave on the morning of day 4.

Hint: Night 1 = 8th, Night 2 = 9th, Night 3 = 10th. Check-out = ?
✓ Answer
11th July
Arrive 8 July, 3 nights (8th, 9th, 10th) → Check out on 11th July.
3c

Abida turns seven on 11th September. Jim turns seven on 18th September. How many days older is Abida?

Both turn 7 in September. Abida's birthday is first.
How many days are between 11th Sept and 18th Sept?

✓ Answer
Abida is 7 days older
18 − 11 = 7 days. Abida was born 7 days before Jim, so she is 7 days older.

Q3d & Q4a — Birthdays & Days Between

Working with years and large date intervals.

3d

Stefania will be 13 years old on 7th October 2023. In what year was she born? How old will she be on 7th October 2030?

Part 1: If she is 13 in 2023, subtract to find her birth year.
Part 2: If she was born that year, how old in 2030?

Hint: Birth year = 2023 − 13. Then 2030 − birth year = age in 2030.
✓ Answer
Born in 2010  ·  Age 20 in 2030
2023 − 13 = 2010 (birth year).   2030 − 2010 = 20 years old.

The orange date marks 3rd June (start — not counted), the pink date marks 25th November (end — not counted). The green dates in between are what we count.

Q4b & Q4c — Ages & Holiday Lengths

Working with birth years and comparing durations.

4b

Daniel was born on 10th January 2003. How old will he be on his birthday in 2018?

To find someone's age: subtract their birth year from the current year.

Hint: 2018 − 2003 = ?
✓ Answer
15 years old
2018 − 2003 = 15. Daniel will be 15 on his birthday in 2018.
4c

Lily's holiday: 9th May – 21st May.
Song's holiday starts 12th May and lasts 2 weeks.
Whose holiday is longer — and by how many days?

Lily: Count the days between 9th and 21st May.
Song: 2 weeks = 14 days.

Hint: Days between 9th and 21st May — do not count the 9th and 21st themselves.
✓ Answer
Song's holiday is longer by 2 days
Lily: 9–21 May = 12 days (May 10–20).   Song: 2 weeks = 14 days.   14 − 12 = 2 days longer for Song.

Questions
5a

I packed a suitcase on 7th April to visit my grandma in three days' time. I will stay for nine days. What day and date will I get home?

Step 1: I leave in 3 days from April 7th → depart on April 10th.
Step 2: I stay for 9 days starting April 10th → arrive home on April 19th.

Hint: 7 + 3 = depart day. Depart day + 9 = home day. Find the day of the week!
✓ Answer
Saturday, 19th April
Leave Apr 10 (Thursday). Stay 9 days: Apr 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 17, 18 → home on 19th April. Looking at the calendar: 19th is a Saturday.
5b

My friend was coming to stay on 16th April for three days. She was ill and couldn't come for another week. Then she stayed for 5 days. What day and date did she go home?

Step 1: Original date 16th + 1 week = new arrival date.
Step 2: New arrival + 5 days = she goes home.

Hint: 16 + 7 = new start. New start + 5 = go home date. Find the day of the week!
✓ Answer
Monday, 28th April
New arrival: 16 + 7 = 23rd April. Stays 5 days: 23, 24, 25, 26, 27 → goes home 28th April. Looking at the calendar: 28th is a Monday.

Question 6 — The Rollercoaster!

Think carefully — both children need to be old enough!

6

Only children aged 10 and above can ride the rollercoaster. Nabil is 8 years old and Gina is 9 years old. How many years do they have to wait until they can both ride together?

Nabil: is 8 years old → needs to be 10 → must wait 2 years.
Gina: is 9 years old → needs to be 10 → must wait 1 year.

For them to ride together, we need to wait until both are old enough.
Who takes longer to reach 10?

Key word: BOTH. They can only ride together when the youngest (Nabil) is also 10. That decides the wait time.
✓ Answer
Wait 2 years
Nabil (8) needs 2 years to reach 10. Gina (9) only needs 1 year, but they must wait for Nabil too. After 2 years: Nabil = 10 ✓   Gina = 11 ✓   → Both can ride! 🎢
